World-Class Funding Platform

Vestinit is a world-class funding platform that uses a set of rigorous investor-ready scrutiny methods for entrepreneurs such as business planning and education, due diligence, quality assurance and legal pressure testing. This methodology is applied to ensure that the business is scalable, that management and support infrastructures are in place, and that the risks are managed. Only entrepreneurs and start-ups that pass the investor ready program will be approved and presented to the investors for ‘Series A’ funding.

Supports a wide range of investors

Vestinit will be available to sophisticated and institutional investors. The platform will cater to both regional and international markets, with the option to white-label at the local level.

Funding for the better

FOR INVESTORS

Vestinit will provide a number of investment options to manage risk and monitor returns based on the client’s risk appetite. The expected returns will be in line with market’s expectations for each of the market categories.

The ROI is dependent on several factors, over a given period. Expected ROI differ with each opportunity depending on the risk, the capital investment, the growth phase of the company that’s invested into, and the risk level associated with a particular fund’s investment profile.

For peer-to-peer lenders, Vestinit provides them with a listing of high-quality investments and a diverse range of options to spread their money wisely and lessen risks for defaults.

FOR ENTREPRENEURS

Vestinit enables potentially successful businesses and startups to scale up and prepare their business models for 'Series A' round of funding through Vestinit's screening methodology, allowing them to elevate into a higher level of professionalism. Vestinit helps entrepreneurs ensure that the necessary documentation, management and support infrastructure is in place, that the business is scalable and the risks are managed.
